Privacy Policy

This policy explains what personal data Make Your Choice collects, how it is used, and your rights in relation to it. This site is operated by David Jones ("Make Your Choice", "we", "us"). If you have any questions, you can contact us at makeyourchoice.dot.blog@gmail.com.

This policy was last updated on 20 March 2026.

What data we collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate this site.

Email address. If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email address is collected and stored by Buttondown, our email service provider. We use this solely to send you notifications of new posts on Make Your Choice. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party for marketing purposes.

IP address. When you subscribe to the newsletter, your IP address is passed to Buttondown as part of the subscription process. This is used by Buttondown for list quality and spam prevention purposes, in accordance with their own privacy policy.

We do not currently run any analytics software and do not track your behaviour on this site beyond what is described above.

Cookies

This site does not set any cookies of its own. Buttondown may set a cookie if you interact with the newsletter subscription form. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

Third-party services

This site uses the following third-party services, each of which operates under their own privacy policy:

Buttondown — our email newsletter service. Stores subscriber email addresses and sends outbound emails on our behalf.

Vercel — our hosting provider. Serves this website and processes serverless function requests including newsletter subscriptions.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

The right to access. You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

The right to erasure. You can request that we delete your personal data. If you are a newsletter subscriber, you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email we send you, which will remove your data from Buttondown. You can also contact us directly to request deletion.

The right to rectification. If any data we hold about you is inaccurate, you can request that we correct it.

The right to object. You can object to our processing of your personal data at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at makeyourchoice.dot.blog@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to the newsletter. If you unsubscribe, your data is deleted from Buttondown in accordance with their data retention policy. We do not retain any other personal data.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. We will not notify subscribers of minor changes, but significant changes to how we handle your data will be communicated by email where possible.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at makeyourchoice.dot.blog@gmail.com.

If you are unhappy with how we have handled your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), the UK's data protection regulator, at ico.org.uk.
